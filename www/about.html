About Us

Ten and counting...

Cafe Arcadia first opened its doors in 2009. The ten years since have seen the small business thrive in our corner of Clerkenwell.

Caffeinated Journey

Coffee houses originated in the Middle East, one of the first places coffee was grown. As they were primarily a place of political gatherings in the early 1500s, imams banned them, and coffee itself due to concern of challenge to the current rule.
